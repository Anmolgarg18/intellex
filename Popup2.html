  • Insight into an executive’s personality and learning agility that can predict occupational success
  • It measures the “bright side” personality - how people relate to others when they are at their best

WORK COMPETENCIES

  • Work Competencies are an individual's strengths and preferences in making things happen at work
  • These are innate talents that influence your effectiveness across roles
  • Understanding them helps you focus on tasks and environments where you perform at your best

VALUES AND MOTIVES

  • The motivations and core values that drive workplace behavior and the culture they align with
  • They describe personality from the inside, what we desire and strive to attain
  • They determine how well suited an individual will be in a particular role or organization
